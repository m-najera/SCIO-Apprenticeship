About

This is a little calculator that let's you find out which of 2 unit setups will deal the most damage to an enemy.

The results are just an aproximation. For more detailed information about the damage calculation please visit EXVIUS WIKI

How to use

You need to input the stats of your unit and the stats of the enemy you are facing.

ATK/MAG

Your unit's ATTACK if your unit deals physical damage.

Your unit's MAGIC if your unit deals magical damage.

BONUS%

Your unit's bonus killers for the type of damage they deal.

ELEMENTS

The element(s) of your weapon(s) if your unit deals physical damage.

Or the element(s) of your spell if your unit deals magical damage.

DEF/SPR

The enemy's DEFENSE if your unit deals physical damage.

The enemy's SPIRIT if your unit deals magical damage.

If you don't know the enemy's DEF/SPR, you can let it at 1. This is manly to get an aproximation of the damage you would deal.

RESISTANCES

Here goes each of your enemy's resitances. If the enemy is weak to an element the value is negative (-100)
